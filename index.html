Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

High-Performance Extrusion

Advanced single-screw extruders melt and homogenize HDPE resin at precise temperatures, ensuring uniform melt flow and consistent pipe properties.

  • Precision temperature control
  • Uniform melt pressure

Controlled Cooling Process

Water spray cooling tanks rapidly and uniformly cool the pipe to lock in dimensional stability and prevent warping or deformation.

  • Uniform radial cooling
  • Stress-free solidification

Precision Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity throughout.

  • Diameter tolerance ±0.1mm
  • Consistent wall thickness

Rigorous Quality Control

Every batch undergoes dimensional, hydrostatic, and impact testing to meet IS 4984, ISO 4427, and customer specifications.

  • 100% hydrostatic pressure test
  • IS 4984 & ISO 4427 compliant

Accurate Pipe Marking

Inkjet printing systems apply permanent markings including pipe size, pressure rating, material grade, and batch traceability codes.

  • Full traceability codes
  • UV-resistant ink

Precision Cutting

Automated cutting stations produce square, burr-free ends at exact lengths to ensure compatibility with fittings and easy field installation.

  • Automated length control
  • Burr-free square cuts

Safe & Efficient Packaging

  • UV-protected wrapping
  • Clearly labelled bundles
